Our Journey

It all started in Hawassa, where a boy saw that strong stories were quietly dying out without being sold. Then came a girl from Mekele, who saw the same problem: brands with heart, ideas with potential, but no voice that could reach far enough. From two corners of Ethiopia, we pursued a common question: "What if the right message could change everything?" That query took us across borders, to Rwanda, Uganda, and Kenya, where we discovered the same quiet behind outstanding ideas. So we created La Monje. Not only to make noise, but to create systems that carry purposeful tales, assist brands in finding their people, and transform attention into influence. We design visibility and engineer relevance.
